Why Choose Dear Diary?

Hiring an event content creator means you get to relive your celebration through the moments that matter most, without waiting weeks for photos or feeling like the day was spent posing. While your guests stay present and your photographer focuses on professional imagery, Dear Diary captures the in-between: the spontaneous laughter, the happy tears, the behind-the-scenes, and the little details you didn’t even notice in real time. You’ll receive raw, beautifully curated content made for sharing and saving, so your memories don’t just live in your mind, but in a visual diary you can revisit instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Dear Diary & Photographers/Videographers?

Dear Diary isn’t here to replace your photographer or videographer, it’s here to complete the team. While they capture the polished highlights of your day, we focus on organic storytelling made for your socials. You’ll receive beautifully curated deliverables so you can share your celebration instantly, repost your vendor team effortlessly, and stay fully present; the final layer made for the way we relive moments today.

What type of events do you cover?

Dear Diary is made for social celebrations: weddings, civil ceremonies, engagements, baptisms, first communions, baby showers, birthdays, and all the milestones worth remembering. We currently focus on social events (not corporate coverage).

Can I add additional coverage?

Yes. Additional coverage can be added subject to availability. Extra hours are billed at $66/hour (minimum 1 hour) and can be booked in advance or requested on the day of the event. We’ll always confirm everything with you before extending.
