The Girl Behind the Diary

Hi, I’m Cristi, the one behind Dear Diary. ♡

A social event content creator born and based in Guatemala.

I’m 21 years old and in my final year studying Design, with a minor in Technology and Communication at Universidad de Palermo in Argentina.

For as long as I can remember, I’ve loved preserving memories through creativity, from art and photography to fashion.

Over the past few months, I’ve worked as a content manager and secondary event planner, developing a deep understanding of the balance between live, organic moments and digital storytelling.

That is where my love for social event content creation truly began, translating the heartfelt emotions of a special day into something you can feel again through a screen.

On your special day, I’m not just your content creator. Think of me as part of your team, capturing authentic moments and turning them into your own visual diary.
